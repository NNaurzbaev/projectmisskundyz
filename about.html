Our Story

Our Vision

Founded in 2015 by art enthusiasts Clara Bennett and David Rosenthal, ArtLens Gallery emerged from a shared passion for making contemporary art accessible to everyone.

We believe art should be an experience, a conversation starter, and a mirror to society's evolving cultural landscape. Our mission is to bridge the gap between artists and art lovers, creating meaningful connections through curated exhibitions and educational programs.

Today, we represent over 50 emerging and established artists from around the world, with a focus on innovative techniques and thought-provoking concepts.
